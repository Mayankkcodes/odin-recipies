Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a warm and comforting dish made with rich layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella. It’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Each layer is packed with flavor, baked to perfection, and best served fresh out of the oven with a sprinkle of Parmesan on top.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil, cook onions and garlic until fragrant, then add ground beef. Brown the meat and stir in tomato sauce and seasonings.
  3. In a baking dish, layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers until the dish is full.
  4. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake at 180 °C (350 °F) for about 45 minutes.
  6. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
